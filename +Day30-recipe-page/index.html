Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

Creamy Chocolate Fudge is a rich and decadent dessert that is perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth cravings. Made with simple ingredients like chocolate, butter, sugar, and condensed milk, this fudge is smooth and creamy with a deep chocolate flavor.

To make Creamy Chocolate Fudge, start by melting chocolate and butter together in a saucepan. Once melted, add in the condensed milk and sugar and stir until the mixture is smooth and well combined. Pour the mixture into a lined pan and let it set in the fridge for a few hours until firm.

The end result is a delicious treat that is perfect for sharing with friends and family or indulging in when you need a little pick-me-up. Creamy Chocolate Fudge is sure to become a favorite dessert in your household, so be sure to make a big batch to satisfy your sweet cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts or other add-ins (optional)

Steps

  1. Line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the sweetened condensed milk, chocolate chips, and butter over low heat.
  3. Stir constantly until the chocolate and butter are melted and the mixture is smooth.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract and chopped nuts or add-ins, if using.
  5. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until the fudge is set.
  7. Once set, lift the fudge out of the pan using the parchment paper overhang and cut into squares.
  8. Serve and enjoy your creamy chocolate fudge!
